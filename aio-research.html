Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is an AIO Blueprint?

+

The AIO Blueprint is a complete technical package we build and install that helps AI systems (Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and others) understand your business. It includes structured data markup, optimized FAQ content, service descriptions, and everything else AI systems need to recognize and recommend your business.

Do I need technical knowledge?

+

No. We handle everything. We research your business, build the complete AIO Blueprint, and deploy it to your live website. You don't need to touch code, learn new tools, or manage any technical implementation. We do all the work.

How quickly will I see results?

+

Most businesses see AI visibility improvements within 2-4 weeks after deployment. AI systems continuously crawl and re-index websites, so the Blueprint starts working immediately once it's live. The exact timeline depends on how frequently AI systems visit your site.

What if I already have SEO?

+

The AIO Blueprint complements existing SEO. They work together. Most SEO agencies don't address AI visibility because it's a newer problem. Your current SEO helps with Google search rankings; the AIO Blueprint helps with AI systems like ChatGPT and Google AI Overviews. Together they cover more ground.
