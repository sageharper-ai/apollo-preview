Two homepage issues worth surfacing first

IssueWhat we foundWhy it matters
Homepage <title> is "| Morgan Keller" The homepage title tag renders as " | Morgan Keller" — the leading variable that should populate the page name appears to be missing or empty in the page template. Title is the single most important on-page SEO element. Currently Google sees a title beginning with a pipe character. A proper title like "Morgan-Keller Construction — Commercial General Contractor in Frederick, MD" would meaningfully improve click-through and ranking signals.
Homepage H1 is empty The homepage has exactly one H1 tag (good — no duplicates), but it contains no text. The current visible heading "Our Reputation Is Building Every Day" is wrapped as an H2. H1 carries strong topical relevance signal. An empty H1 means Google has to derive the page topic from less-weighted elements. Setting H1 to either the brand-line or a service-anchored phrase recovers this signal.

Title length distribution (66 of 100 pages over 60 chars)

Most over-long titles come from the /news/ section, where article titles plus the " | Morgan Keller" suffix push past Google's ~60-character SERP display limit. This is cosmetic — Google still indexes the full title, but the SERP display gets truncated. A small adjustment to the news-page title template (drop the brand suffix on news pages, or shorten article titles) would fix it.

Excellent fundamentals worth keeping

  • Alt-text coverage at 100% — 109 of 109 images have alt attributes. Many sites this size are at 60–80%. This is exemplary discipline.
  • Single H1 per page (where present) — no duplicate H1s, no missing H1s on subpages. The empty homepage H1 is the only outlier.
  • Sitemap and robots.txt both present — sitemap.xml is published and accessible.

What we submitted vs. what people actually search

Two patterns worth surfacing:

  • healthcare facility construction maryland (Competitor Pressure 63) — heaviest organic competition in the set. State-level keyword dominated by national GCs. Address with a Maryland-positioning content piece, not a service page.
  • school construction frederick county md (Competitor Pressure 46) — institutional / public-sector niche. Frederick County Public Schools is the obvious primary buyer. A dedicated landing page with project case studies (Hagerstown CC Learning Resource Center, MSM Knott Academic Center) would earn this query.

Why two keywords returned "No Data Returned"

Two keywords (medical office buildings near me and commercial contractors frederick md) were submitted to the keyword research tool and saved in the project but did not return data when the table was queried. Likely causes: "near me" geo-aware queries are handled differently by the data source's backend, and plural variants are sometimes deduplicated against their singular form. We're flagging these explicitly rather than reporting zero — both keywords are still worth tracking.

Next Steps

Three things Morgan-Keller can do this month — concrete, in-house, free or low-cost.

1. Fix the homepage <title> tag and empty H1

Both are template-level fixes that take roughly 30 minutes of dev time. Title is the single most important on-page SEO element; the current "| Morgan Keller" rendering means Google sees a title beginning with a pipe character. A proper title like "Morgan-Keller Construction — Commercial General Contractor in Frederick, MD" would meaningfully improve click-through and ranking signals. Setting H1 to either the brand-line or a service-anchored phrase recovers the strongest topical relevance signal Google uses.

2. Add meta descriptions to the 18 pages currently missing them

Most are /news/ entries; meta descriptions can be templated from the article excerpt. Meta descriptions don't affect ranking directly but they dramatically affect click-through rate from the SERP — and pages without one let Google auto-generate a snippet that often misses the buyer-intent angle.

3. Audit the highest-volume keyword in the set

"commercial construction frederick md" (10 monthly searches, low Competitor Pressure) is the single keyword with both real volume and a winnable competitive position. Morgan-Keller currently ranks #2 organic for it. Reviewing what currently sits at #1 in the SERP and identifying the on-page signal differences — content depth, internal linking, structured headings — is the lowest-effort path to claiming the #1 organic position for the keyword that already drives the most local search volume in the category.

Methodology notes

  • Local SEO methodology: All keyword research used city-phrase keywords ("frederick md") with county-level location filtering (Frederick County, MD). County names are not searched as queries; city names are. Uniform application across all Apollo SEO clients.
  • No US-wide data was used. US-wide search volume inflates expectations and breaks trust with local-service buyers. Every metric in this report is filtered to Frederick County, MD or the explicit state-level keyword (where indicated).
  • Honest reporting on gaps: Two keywords returned no data from the research source and are flagged as such. Backlink 30-day velocity is rendered as an interactive chart rather than a discrete number by the data source — flagged Incomplete rather than approximated.
